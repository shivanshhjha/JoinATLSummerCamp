ABOUT US

Atal Tinker Labs(ATL) is an Initiative of the NITI Aayog, Govt. of India Under the Atal Innovation Mission. With a vision to 'Cultivate one Million children in India as Neoteric Innovators'.

Young children will get a chance to work with tools and equipment to understand what, how, and why aspects of STEM(Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math). ATL Summer camps are a way to help the young generation gain knowledge in areas of their interest during the Summer vacation and utilize their time in building a constructive thought process.

Here, we come with the ATL summer camp at AIC Raise where you can try hands-on Do-it-yourself mode and get to learn innovation skills under the experts. ATL Summer camp is a workspace where young minds and their ideas can be shaped, encouraged, and cultivated in a more gratifying manner.
